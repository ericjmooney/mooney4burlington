About Katie

Committed to serving our community

Katie Mooney is running for Alderman of District 3 in Burlington, Wisconsin because she believes in the power of local government to make a real difference in people's lives.

As a longtime resident of Burlington, Katie understands the issues that matter most to our community. She's committed to listening to residents, working collaboratively with fellow council members, and making decisions that put the needs of District 3 first.

Katie believes that good governance means transparency, fiscal responsibility, and always keeping an open door for constituents who want their voices heard.
